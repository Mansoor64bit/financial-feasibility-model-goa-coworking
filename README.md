# Financial Feasibility Analysis of a Coworking Space in Goa

## Project Overview

This project evaluates the financial feasibility of establishing a coworking space in Goa through a comprehensive Excel-based financial model.

The model analyzes occupancy, revenue generation, operating costs, profitability, cash flows, investment returns, and business sustainability under multiple operating scenarios.

---

## Objectives

- Evaluate financial feasibility of a coworking space business
- Compare owned and leased property models
- Determine break-even occupancy levels
- Assess profitability under different scenarios
- Analyze investment attractiveness using capital budgeting techniques
- Evaluate financial risks and business sustainability

## Project Assumptions

- Seating Capacity: 100 Seats
- Total Area: 5,000 sq. ft.
- Projection Period: 5 Years
- Occupancy Scenarios:
  - 40%
  - 60%
  - 80%
